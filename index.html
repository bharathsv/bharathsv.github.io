I am a Professor in the Department of Statistics and Department of Mathematics (by courtesy) at the Pennsylvania State University.

My research interests include
  • non-parametric statistics
  • machine learning, statistical learning theory
  • optimal transport and gradient flows
  • reproducing kernel Hilbert spaces, regularization theory, inverse problems
  • functional and topological data analysis

My current research is supported by the award NSF-DMS-CAREER-1945396
"Statistical Learning, Inference and Approximation with Reproducing Kernels"
