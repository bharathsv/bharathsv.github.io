Stability Analysis of Linear Time-Invariant Systems  (B. Tech Project, Dept. of ECE, Sri Venkateswara University, India)
The stability analyzers of linear time-invariant (LTI) control systems such as Routh-Hurwitz criterion, Root-Locus plots, Bode and Nyquist plots are investigated and implemented to analyze the absolute and relative stabilities of a control system, given its transfer function. A software package is developed which conducts the above mentioned tests of stability and provides the appropriate plots. The nature of stability and stability criterion are provided for a given transfer function of a linear LTI control system.
